International Degrees

In order to convalidate a foreign degree, Santa Clara University requires that the student provides an official transcript of his/hers degree. This official transcript must be performed by a member of the National Association of Credential Evaluation Services (NACES). A list of the organization members and a link to their websites is avaiable in the Links and forms section. Although some organizations may include copies of the document, Santa Clara requires an original document for its validation.

You can click on the pictures to view a sample of what the document must contain. It is not mandatory that it follows this exact format.

The transcript must fulfill certain conditions in order to be accepted. Although most of the mentioned organizations are aware of these conditions, we provide them here so that you can verify the given fields once you receive your copy. The document must include:

  • Course-by-course translation.
  • Cumulative GPA
  • U.S. degree equivalency.
