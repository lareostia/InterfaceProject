International Degrees.

In order to convalidate a foreign degree, Santa Clara University requires that the student provides an official transcript of his/hers degree. This official transcript must be performed by any memeber of the National Association of Credential Evaluation Services (NACES). There is available a list of the organization members and their web pages on the helpful links section. Although some organizations may include copies of the document, Santa Clara requires an original document for its validation. Here is provided an EXAMPLE of what the document should CONTAIN. It is not mandatory that it follows this exact format.

The transcript must fulfill certain conditions in order to be accepted. Although most of the mentioned organizations are aware of these conditions, we provide them here so that you can verify the given fields once you receive your copy. The document must include:

  • Course-by-course translation.
  • Cumulative GPA
  • U.S. degree equivalency.
