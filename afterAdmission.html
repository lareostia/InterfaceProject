After Admission

Congratulations on being accepted at Santa Clara University. The following checklist will help you organise the process.
Step 1 : You will need a document called the I-20 from the university to be issued a visa. The following documents are required to to receive the I20 from the university. Deadlines for requesting the I-20 form

  • I-20 Request Form
  • Financial Documents
  • Affidavit of Support
  • Biographical page of your Passport

These documents must be mailed to : Mail to admissions dept

Step 2 : After you receive the I-20 from the university, it is time to apply for a Visa at the US embassy/consulate in your country. You must complete the online visa application form

  • Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application :DS-160
  • Photo

Step 3 :Documents you need to preparing for the Inteview

  • Application fee payment receipt
  • Nonimmigrant Visa Application, Form DS-160 confirmation page
  • Certificate of Eligibility for Nonimmigrant (F-1) Student Status-For Academic and Language Students, Form I-20A-B
  • Passport valid for travel to the U. S
  • printed photo , if the photo upload fails while filling form DS-160
