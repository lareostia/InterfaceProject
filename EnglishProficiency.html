English proficiency

If your first language is not English, you will need to take any of the Standard tests listed below to prove proficiency in the English language to attend and comprehend the lectures and classes taught by the university. This requirement will apply to every International aplicant regardless of nationality

The University requires that you have either a TOEFL or an IELTS language certifications. .

Sample pictures of a TOEFL and IELTS score card to help you recognise which document you need:

Minimum TOEFL or IELTS scores that an applicant must obtain in order to get accepted into the university :

  • Minimum TOEFL score: IBT/79-80 or CBT: 213
  • Minimum IELTS score: 6.5
