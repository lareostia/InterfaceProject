English proficiency

In case that the prospective student's first language is not English, in order to get admitted to the university, you have to proove that you have a sufficient level in English language to attend and comprehend the lectures and classes tought by the university. The way to do this is by obtaining either a TOEFL or an IELTS language certifications. This requirement will apply to every aplicat regardless of nationality.

There are minimum TOEFL or IELTS scores that an applicant must obtain in order to get accepted into the university. These scores are:

  • Minimum TOEFL score: IBT/79-80 or CBT: 213
  • Minimum IELTS score: 6.5
