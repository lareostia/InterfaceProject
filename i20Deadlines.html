Deadlines for requesting the I-20 from the university

For the Winter 2014 quarter: the deadline for initial I-20s/DS-2019s is December 8, 2013 and for transfer students: January 2, 2014.
For the Spring 2014 quarter:the deadline for initial I-20s/DS-2019s is March 1, 2014 and for transfer students: March 24, 2014.
