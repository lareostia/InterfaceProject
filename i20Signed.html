Form I-20

The I-20 is a form that that you receive from the University you are admitted in.
It contains the university name, the course you have applied to and your personal details like name and date of birth.

The I-20 must be duly signed by the student and taken for the Visa interview.
The I-20 will also be screened for a valid signature on arrival at the port of entry to the US
