Applying for a change of status.

In case you fulfill the conditions to be eligible for a change of status, you are required to fill up the document I-539. This document should be submitted to the US authorities 54 days prior the expiration of the I-94 permit.

You have two options for submitting this document: by common mail or performing an electronic submission. In the Forms and links section we provide the link for electronic submission or the address that you must mail your request depending on your current location. If your application is approved you will later receive an I-797. Change of status students will need to check in with a copy of the I-797 approval notice upon receipt of the approval.
