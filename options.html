Visa process assistance

  • Prior to Admission

    This section provides a list of documents required to apply to Santa Clara University. Helpful links and contact information is provided to assist the Student through the application process.

  • After Admission

    After being admitted at Santa Clara University, there are several steps that each international student must take to apply for a student visa. This section offers a checklist of documents required to apply for the Student Visa. Helpful links and links to some of the forms required during this step of the process are provided for each document necessary document.

  • Entering USA

    After obtaining a Student Visa, the student must have a set of documents to permit entry to the United States of America. This section lists the documents the student will need to produce at the Port of Entry to USA and on arrival at Santa Clara University.
