• Prior to admission.
    In this section you can go through the necessary documentation to apply to Santa Clara University. It also provides helpful links and contacts to assist the applier through the various doubts and questions that may come up during the process.
  • After admission.
    After being admitted at Santa Clara University, there are several steps that each international student must take to apply for a student visa. In this section we provide a detailed description of those steps, necessary documents and helpful contact links and emails that the students can use if needed so.
  • Entering the country.
    At the time of entering the country, each student must be carrying a few documents that he will be required to show in the border. In this section we provide a list of those documents and the possible questions each student might be asked.
