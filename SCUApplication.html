SCU application.

In order to get admitted into SCU you need to fill out an online application form. This form can be submitted online in the SCU link provided in this page. It is required an email address in order to create an account.

If you so desire, you can also mail a hard copy of your application to the following address:

Engineering Graduate Admissions
School of Engineering
Santa Clara University
500 El Camino Real
Santa Clara, CA 95053-0583
