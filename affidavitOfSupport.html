Affidavit of Support

This form is required of all admitted students requesting an I-20/DS-2019 immigration form. The University is required by U.S. federal regulations to obtain documentation proving that you have current resources available to support yourself (and your dependents, if applicable) during your first year of study at SCU. You can download the form below from the Forms and Links section.
